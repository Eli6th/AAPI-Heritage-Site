    AAPI History Week

    (April 28-May 6)

    Stay Posted

    Event Details

    The week will spotlight the film Far East Deep South, a documentary about the journey of a Chinese American family traveling south to uncover their roots. In the process, the family uncovers buried evidence of discrimination.

    Viewers will be able to experience historical events such as the Chinese Exclusion Act along with the people at the time. The film has won several awards including the “Best Documentary at the CineOdyssey'' and “Coronado Island Film Festivals”.

    Throughout the week we will not only explore the role of AAPI in US history but also investigate the depth of AAPI oppression which can still be found in today’s world.
