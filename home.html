    The AAPI Heritage Month Celebration is a month-long event that aims to celebrate AAPI culture during the month of May. As a community, we found that there were 3 aspects of culture we wanted to celebrate: AAPI History, art, and food. Each week of May will feature a different aspect of AAPI culture, with the first-week highlighting AAPI history, the second week, art, and the third week, food. The final week will feature a festival featuring food, music, and games following the AAPI 5k Run N' Walk.
